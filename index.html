  • March '16

    Getting Going...

    Here at Go, we want more people to get active, by doing things they want to do with people nearby. With our aims set out, we incorporated our Company, SalmonScandal Ltd. and got Going from there.

  • August '16

    Designs: Done; Development: Begun.

    Beta designs wrapped up, and development begins soon after. We're dynamically updating GoApp to make sure that even the beta release in Jan '17 is a joy to use.

  • January '17

    STOP. Beta Time

    This is most certainly something you can touch. Sign up below to be the first to Get Going. We welcome all kinds of feedback, so we really can make an app for the people. Once we've heard your calls, it's back to the drawing board, until....

  • Summer '17

    Hard Launch

    GoApp will be released in June 2017. It'll be in the best shape it's ever been in; and once you start using it, you will too.
